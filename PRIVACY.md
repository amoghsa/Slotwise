# Slotwise — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-05-30
**Last updated:** 2026-05-30

Slotwise (iOS and Android) is designed around a single promise: **your data
never leaves your device.** This policy explains, plainly, what that means.

## The short version

- **No accounts.** No sign-up, login, username, password, or server-side profile.
- **No data collection.** We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your
  information. There are no analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising, and no
  third-party SDKs that phone home in the current version.
- **No network use.** The app functions entirely offline. The only time data
  leaves the app is when *you* explicitly export a calendar file and choose where
  to send it via your device's share sheet (see "Exporting your plan" below).

## What data the app stores (on your device only)

Everything you enter is stored locally in the app's private storage:

- Your household name, time zone, and optional home label
- Your kids' names, colors, and optional emoji
- Seasons and their date ranges
- Family rules (limits, cut-off times, no-activity days, gaps)
- Activities, their candidate time slots, locations, and prep/travel times
- Which slot you chose for each activity

This data stays in the app's sandbox. It is not uploaded anywhere. We — the
developers — never see it.

## Exporting your plan

When you tap **Export**, Slotwise generates a standard calendar file (`.ics`) and
hands it to your operating system's share sheet. **You** then decide what to do
with it — import it into Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, email it to yourself,
etc. Once you send that file to another app or service, *that* app's privacy
terms apply to the copy you sent. Slotwise itself does not transmit the file.

## Permissions

Slotwise requests **no special permissions** in the current version — no
contacts, no location, no calendar write access, no storage permission, and no
network permission beyond what the OS share sheet uses on your behalf.

## Children's privacy

Although the app is *about* scheduling children's activities, it is used by a
parent/caregiver and collects nothing about anyone — adult or child — on any
server. All names and details you type stay on your device.

## Changes to this policy

If a future version adds any feature that sends data off the device (for example,
optional cloud backup), we will update this policy and clearly disclose it in the
app **before** any such feature is enabled. We will not silently add tracking.
